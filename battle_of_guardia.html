The Battle of Guardia

The Battle of Guardia is considered the most catastrophic conflict in the modern era — the day David Spike, corrupted by Void energy, launched a full‑scale assault on Guardia City, forcing Seron Darkness into a battle that shook the foundations of the galaxy.

Prelude to War

Once a member of the Golden Trio, David Spike fell into corruption after exposure to the Void Rift. His mind fractured, his power multiplied, and his purpose twisted into destruction.

When David marched on Guardia with an army of Void‑corrupted creatures, the Guardians were overwhelmed instantly.

The Invasion

David’s forces tore through the city. Buildings collapsed. Streets split open. Civilians fled as the sky turned black with Void energy.

The Guardians fought desperately — Connor Violet, Kole Rogue, Riven Solari, Tom Letanio, Wave, and many others — but David was unstoppable.

Only one person could face him directly.

Seron vs David Spike

The duel between Seron and David became the centerpiece of the battle — a clash of cosmic power and corrupted Void energy.

Their fight tore through districts, shattered the sky, and warped the battlefield. Seron fought to save his friend. David fought to destroy everything he once loved.

The emotional weight of the battle was immense — two brothers in all but blood, forced to fight to the death.

The Turning Point

When David unleashed his full Void form, Seron was forced to ascend into his Dark Star State. The resulting explosion of energy purified the Void corruption long enough for the Guardians to intervene.

David collapsed — defeated, but alive.

Aftermath

Guardia City was left in ruins. The Guardians were nearly wiped out. The Golden Trio was shattered forever.

But the galaxy survived — because Seron refused to let his friend die a monster.
