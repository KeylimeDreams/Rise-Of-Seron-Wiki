About the universe

The Rise Of Seron chronicles an omniversal god named Seron “Super” Darkness, the main hero and the original creator of both The ROS universe and The Zavila. From the first spark of existence to the farthest edges of the omniverse, Seron shapes reality itself.

As the architect of galaxies and the guardian of balance, Seron stands against forces that threaten to consume creation in pure void. His power is not just destructive darkness, but a controlled, intelligent shadow that shields what he has made.

The Guardians

To protect the universe and the Zavila galaxy, Seron formed an elite order known as The Guardians. Each Guardian carries a fragment of Seron’s will—tasked with defending worlds, stabilizing cosmic rifts, and confronting entities that even gods fear to name.

Across galaxies, their presence is felt as emerald flares in the dark: a signal that Seron’s watchful gaze is never truly gone, even when the stars themselves fall silent.
