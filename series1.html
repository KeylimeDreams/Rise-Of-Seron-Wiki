The Zavila Galaxy Series 1

Series 1 is the origin season of the Zavila Galaxy saga, containing 11 major stories that establish the universe’s early conflicts, character awakenings, and cosmic threats.

Stories Included

1. A Stranger I Remain — Kauko Roni Kulmala and Alex Chronos uncover the Shadow Samurai kingdom and the prophecy of the Shadow Heir.

2. Arsonist’s Lullabye — Kole Rogue confronts his unstable Seron fragment, his past, and the trauma that defines him.

3. Pierce Vs Crimson — Angelo Pierce faces Falor Crimson, the Decaying King, in a tragic confrontation.

4. Stranded On A Desert Planet — Connor, Kole, Shifter, and Wave crash on Vor’Kar and face survival horror.

5. The Battle of Guardia — Part 1 — David Spike invades Guardia, beginning the first large-scale war.

6. The Battle of Guardia — Part 2 — Reinforcements arrive; Seron confronts David Spike in a cosmic duel.

7. The Darkness Surrounds — Royal, Peter, and Esteban explore Noxaria‑5 and encounter the Chromavore.

8. The False Hero — A Seron impostor sparks political tension and conspiracy in Guardia City.

9. The Fate of Dark Phantom — Seron and Casey investigate Zavara’s phantom crisis and the traitor Veylor.

10. The Kacia Desert Vexx Empire Investigation — Riven and Jotaro uncover Vexx Empire secrets and the Heart of Kacia.

11. The Zavara Starline Crash Site — Michael and Sinx discover Angelo’s lost flagship and its corrupted remains.

Season Themes

Identity, trauma, cosmic destiny, the rise of the Guardians, and the first fractures in the galaxy.

Chronological Order

The Fate of Dark Phantom → Pierce Vs Crimson → The Zavara Starline Crash Site → A Stranger I Remain → Arsonist’s Lullabye → The Darkness Surrounds → The False Hero → Stranded On A Desert Planet → Kacia Desert Investigation → Battle of Guardia Part 1 → Battle of Guardia Part 2
