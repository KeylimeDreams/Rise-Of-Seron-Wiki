The Blood Moon's Light

The Blood Moon’s Light follows Marcel Toxin as he travels to OverDarkis to uncover the truth behind the Blood Moon phenomenon. Set after the Zavara Heist storyline and during the Blood Moon events, Marcel’s investigation reveals a threat far older — and far more dangerous — than anyone expected.

1 — Arrival on OverDarkis

While the Guardians attend the Blood Moon Party, Marcel Toxin arrives separately on a research mission. His goal: discover why the Blood Moon manifests and what energy fuels it.

OverDarkis is quiet, its obsidian terrain glowing faintly under the crimson sky. Marcel sets up scanners and begins tracking the lunar energy signature.

2 — The Second Crystal

Marcel’s readings lead him deep into a canyon where the moonlight concentrates unnaturally. There, half‑buried in the stone, he finds it:

A second Blood Moon Crystal.

Unlike the one Tom shattered, this crystal is dormant — but pulsing faintly with corrupted lunar energy. Marcel carefully extracts it and begins analyzing its structure.

The crystal contains traces of Aether… but also something else. Something older.

3 — The Symbol in the Dust

As Marcel brushes away the surrounding dirt, he notices a marking carved into the stone beneath the crystal.

A swirling fog‑shaped insignia.

He scans it, confused. It doesn’t match any known faction, empire, or Aether group.

Marcel: “What… is this?”

He sends the symbol to Seron through a secure channel.

4 — Seron Recognizes the Mark

Seron responds almost immediately.

Seron: “Marcel… that symbol is not supposed to exist.”

Marcel waits as Seron continues.

Seron: “It belongs to the Fogborn Syndicate. A group so old most believe they’re myths. When the fog comes… their members come with it.”

Marcel freezes. The Fogborn Syndicate — whispered about in ancient Guardian records — were said to manipulate fog, shadows, and memory itself.

5 — The Implication

Marcel looks back at the Blood Moon Crystal. If the Fogborn Syndicate had touched it… If they were involved in the Blood Moon’s power…

Then the Blood Moon wasn’t just a natural phenomenon. It was a signal.

A warning. Or a summoning.

6 — Contacting Seron

Marcel sends all his data to Seron — energy readings, crystal scans, and the symbol’s location.

Marcel: “Seron… if the Fogborn Syndicate is real, we’re dealing with something ancient.”

Seron: “Return to HQ. Now. We need to prepare.”

Marcel pockets the crystal and begins his journey back, unaware that a faint fog begins to rise behind him…

The Fogborn Syndicate had awakened.
