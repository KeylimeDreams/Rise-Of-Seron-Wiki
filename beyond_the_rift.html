Beyond The Rift

The Beyond The Rift Mission is one of the most tragic and defining events in the Rise Of Seron universe — the moment the Golden Trio was shattered, David Spike was lost to the Void, and Seron Darkness was secretly infected by Emperor Void.

The Mission Begins

A dimensional rupture opened above Guardia’s East District — a swirling tear of violet and black energy. Reports claimed civilians were being pulled inside. Seron Darkness, Connor Violet, and David Spike responded immediately.

Inside the Rift, time fractured. Gravity twisted. Voices echoed from impossible directions. The trio pushed forward, unaware that Emperor Void was waiting.

David’s Capture

As the team reached the Rift’s core, a massive shockwave separated them. David was thrown into a collapsing corridor of Void energy. Seron and Connor sprinted after him — but they were seconds too late.

Emperor Void emerged from the darkness, seized David by the throat, and dragged him into the deeper layers of the Void Realm.

David’s scream echoed — then vanished.

Corruption and False Memories

In the Void Realm, Emperor Void began reshaping David’s mind. He injected corrupted Aether into David’s bloodstream, rewriting memories, twisting emotions, and planting false betrayals.

David forgot who he was. He forgot Seron. He forgot Connor. He forgot the Golden Trio.

In their place, Emperor Void crafted a new identity — one loyal only to him.

Seron’s Secret Infection

As Seron and Connor escaped the collapsing Rift, a tendril of Void energy lashed out and struck Seron across the face. He didn’t tell Connor. He didn’t think it mattered.

But the infection began immediately.

That night, Seron’s eyes shifted from bright green to a deep, haunting purple — the mark of the Void. He hid it beneath his hood, refusing to show weakness.

The Five Days After

For five days, Seron barely slept. He searched every district, every anomaly, every trace of Void energy. His voice grew colder. His patience thinned. His leadership hardened.

Connor noticed the change — the silence, the distant stare, the purple glow beneath Seron’s eyes. But Seron refused to stop.

He would find David. No matter what the Void had done to him. No matter what it was doing to Seron himself.

Legacy of the Rift

Beyond The Rift marks the moment the Golden Trio was broken — and the moment Seron’s destiny shifted forever. The infection would haunt him, strengthen him, and eventually push him toward the Dark Star State.

And somewhere in the Void, David Spike waited — corrupted, rewritten, and no longer the man Seron once called brother.
