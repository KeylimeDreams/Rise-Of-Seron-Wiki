The Zavila Galaxy: Series 4

Series 4 marks the beginning of the Chronos & Ignis Era, a dual‑saga exploring the fragile tension between the flow of Time and the fury of the Flame. Following the collapse of the Hell Era, the Guardians must recover lost artifacts and confront an ancient entity capable of reformatting the universe itself.

Arc 1 — The Chronos Fracture

Temporal instability spreads across the galaxy as Temporal Stutters begin erasing Guardians from existence. The team must hunt down Chrono‑Artifacts to anchor the present and prevent reality from collapsing.

Story 1 — The Fracture of Time

Kole Rogue is dispatched to the wasteland of Cinder‑9 to retrieve the Solaris Artifact. He faces Lando Braddock and his time‑erasing stand, Scarlet Knight.

Story 2 — The Red Corp Paradox

Connor Violet infiltrates Red Corp Industries to shut down a rogue Chrono‑Engine. He battles Keith Mystery and his spirit Cyan Piercer before arresting Dr. Anthony Red.

Story 3 — The Syndicate’s Gambit

The Fogborn Syndicate launches a direct assault on Guardians HQ. Connor confronts his past with Mindy Kalvor (Banshee), while Miles Church ascends into his Purification Ultimate Form 1 to secure the Ouroboros Gear.

Arc 2 — The Ignis Vortem Awakening

With the timeline stabilized, the Guardians travel to the volcanic world Ignis Vortem. What begins as a resource mission becomes an archaeological race against a force older than history itself.

Story 4 — The Ignis Vortem Awakening (Part 1)

HawkBat Rogue and Casey Vigor investigate a destroyed lava factory and uncover ancient texts describing an entity known as Babylon. The trail leads to the Dark Pit Sector.

Story 5 — The Ignis Vortem Awakening (Part 2)

The duo descends into the Temple of the First Script, where they meet the Ancient Librarian, who warns that reality will break the deeper they go.

Story 6 — The Vault of the False Creator

After defeating the Logos‑Golem, the Guardians secure the Ancient Stasis Pod containing Babylon. This discovery sets the stage for the upcoming Time‑Locked Era.

Era Mechanics & New Elements

The Chrono‑Anchor: Connor Violet’s invention preventing erasure during temporal shifts.

Purification Energy: Miles Church’s rare power that resets corrupted beings.

Script‑Shades: Ink‑based enemies capable of rewriting physical impact within the Dark Pit.

Conclusion

Series 4 concludes with Michael Rogue being sent to Guardia to investigate a mountain disturbance — the first signal of the Time‑Locked Era that follows.
