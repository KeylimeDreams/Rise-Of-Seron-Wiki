About the Universe

Seron “Super” Darkness is the omniversal god, the main hero, and the creator of both The ROS Universe and The Zavila. His power shapes galaxies, stabilizes cosmic forces, and defines the balance between creation and void.

Seron’s darkness is not corruption — it is a controlled, intelligent force that shields existence from annihilation. His influence extends across realities, binding the omniverse together.

The Guardians

To defend the universe, Seron formed The Guardians — an elite order empowered with fragments of his will. They protect worlds, confront cosmic threats, and maintain stability across the Zavila Galaxy.
