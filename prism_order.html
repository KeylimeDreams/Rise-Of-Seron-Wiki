The Prism Order

The Prism Order continues directly after the events of The Zavara Heist. Seron Darkness, Tom Letanio, and Cody Kritz descend deeper into the Zavaran Mines to investigate the hidden laboratory Arsenio Beau Innes opened — a place where horrors, secrets, and betrayals converge.

1 — Into the Lower Mines

The trio follows Arsenio’s trail into the abandoned lower tunnels. Strange lights flicker through the caverns, and the air hums with unstable Aether. Tom senses something wrong — something ancient and engineered.

“This wasn’t built by miners,” Tom mutters. “This is… surgical.”

2 — The Prism Order Lab

They reach a sealed blast door marked with a symbol: a fractured prism. The Prism Order.

Inside, they find containment pods filled with civilians — unconscious, restrained, and infused with unstable Power Aether. The Prism Order had been experimenting on innocent people, forcing Aether mutations to create artificial superhumans.

“This is sick,” Cody growls. “Arsenio wasn’t lying…”

3 — The Vexx Connection

Tom accesses a terminal and discovers encrypted logs. As he decrypts them, his expression darkens.

“Seron… the Prism Order made deals with the Vexx Empire. They traded research. They shared samples. They were trying to replicate Emperor Void’s corruption.”

Cody confirms the worst — Arsenio’s claims were true. The people behind this were not just criminals. They were rogue Guardian members.

4 — Seron Processes the Truth

Seron stands silently, staring at the corrupted Aether tanks. His voice is low, controlled, but shaken.

“If Guardians were involved… this goes deeper than we thought.”

He closes the terminal. “We take everything. Every log. Every sample. We bring this to HQ.”

5 — Reinforcements Arrive

Seron opens a dark rift and calls for backup. Three Guardian Operatives arrive:

Vlasta Jeronim — Logistics & Data Retrieval Specialist (Secretly a Prism Order spy — unknown to the team)

Operator Jaxen Rhymer — Close‑Quarters Combat & Security Operator Selene Ward — Aether Hazard Containment

Together, they secure the lab, extract the civilians, and gather every piece of evidence.

6 — The Beginning of Something Worse

As they leave the collapsing lab, Seron looks back at the Prism Order insignia burning on the wall.

“If the Prism Order and the Vexx Empire worked together… then this is only the beginning.”

Vlasta Jeronim watches silently — already planning her next move.
