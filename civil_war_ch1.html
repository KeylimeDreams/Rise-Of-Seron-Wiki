Chapter 1 — The War Unnoticed

Parts 1–5

Part 1 — Malfunction

Casey Vigor — The Isotope Hero — was supposed to be running a routine Guardian training session. But halfway through the drill, his isotope core began to destabilize. Energy spiked, cracked, and lashed out in violent bursts that scorched the walls and shook the arena.

Wave, The Power Copier, rushed in and shut down the grid before the entire bay collapsed. “Casey, that’s it. You’re done for today. You’re benched until we figure out what’s happening to you.”

Casey didn’t argue. He just lowered his head and walked out — silent, frustrated, and afraid of what he was becoming.

Part 2 — The Crash Site

Hours later, Casey wandered alone through the ruins of the old Vexx Empire Crash Site. Twisted metal, shattered engines, and scorched earth surrounded him — a graveyard of a fallen empire, and a reflection of how he felt inside.

He sat on a broken Vexx wing, staring at his trembling hands. “Why can’t I control this…?”

Part 3 — The Visitors

A ripple of energy tore through the air. Three figures stepped out of a temporal distortion:

Martin Velaot — The 2nd Time Breaker God. David Kaine — The 2nd Arsonist. Viridian Viper — Nova Watch Commander.

“Casey Vigor,” Viridian said calmly. “The Guardians don’t value you. They hide things. They use you. They let you break while they look the other way.”

Martin stepped forward. “Nova Watch can give you stability. Purpose. A place where your power matters.”

Casey hesitated. Their words felt wrong — but they also felt comforting. He didn’t say yes. He didn’t say no. He just stood there, unsure of everything.

Part 4 — The Retrieval Team

Back at Guardian HQ, Seron sensed something was off. Casey’s signal was drifting. Nova Watch operatives were too close. Something wasn’t right.

“Kole. Andrew. With me.”

Kole Rogue — The Original Arsonist — cracked his knuckles, flames flickering across his shoulders. Andrew OverDark — The 1st Time Breaker God — opened a temporal rift.

“We’re bringing Casey home,” Seron said, stepping into the rift.

Part 5 — The Breaking Point

The two groups met in the ruins — Guardians on one side, Nova Watch on the other. Casey stood between them, overwhelmed and terrified.

Viridian Viper stepped forward. “Casey chooses his own path. You don’t own him.”

Kole snarled. “You’re manipulating him. Back off.”

Andrew’s voice echoed with temporal distortion. “This ends now.”

The tension snapped like a blade.

Seron arrived last, stepping through a dark rift. His voice was cold, sharp, and final:

“If Nova Watch wants a war… then I, Super Darkness, declare a Civil War.”
