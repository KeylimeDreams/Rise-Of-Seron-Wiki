The Zavila Galaxy: Series 3

Series 3 chronicles the beginning, rise, and conclusion of the Hell Era — a catastrophic period where the boundaries between the physical universe and the Infernal Dimensions collapsed. The Guardians face multi‑dimensional warfare, political chaos, internal infiltration, and the awakening of an ancient Void‑God.

Arc 1 — The Infernal Compact

The first half of Series 3 follows the activation of Project: Nexus and the Guardians’ descent into the Infernal Dimensions. Their mission: secure alliances, stop the riots tearing through Pandemonium, and uncover the source of the dimensional instability.

Story 1 — The Lucifer Compact

The Guardians travel to Pandemonium to negotiate a fragile alliance with Lucifer Morningstar. During the mission, Tom Letanio’s hidden identity as King Indigo is revealed, reshaping the political landscape of the underworld.

Story 2 — Pandemonium Unchained

Urban warfare erupts across the Pride and Lust Rings. The Guardians battle gangs equipped with Anti‑Chrono tech designed to counter time‑based abilities. Their objective: reach the core of the riots and shut down the destabilizing forces.

Story 3 — The King and the Architect

Tom Letanio confronts his rival, Norman Stillman, in a brutal duel. Pushed to the brink of death, Tom undergoes his first Blue Phoenix Rebirth, annihilating the Architect and ending the riots.

Story 4 — The Voyage Home

The Guardians return to Guardia with Stillman in custody — unaware that a Calamity‑Shade has followed them through the portal and vanished into the shadows of HQ.

Arc 2 — The Shadows of Movkos

The second half of Series 3 focuses on the internal breach of Guardians HQ and the revelation of the true mastermind behind the Hell Era.

Story 5 — The Void Nullifier Heist

Wave, Michael Rogue, Riven Solari, and Lilia Spike infiltrate a Zavara vault to retrieve a Void Nullifier. Activating it exposes the demon hiding inside HQ.

Story 6 — Shadows in the Circuitry

Under amber lockdown, Connor Violet, Casey Vigor, HawkBat Rogue, and Andrew OverDark strategize while Tom identifies the intruder as a Calamity‑Shade feeding on the base’s power grid.

Story 7 — The Exorcism of Guardia

Seron and Tom battle the demon in the reactor core. The entity reveals its true identity: Lord Movkos, an ancient Void‑God. Movkos mortally wounds Seron and nearly destroys the base.

Story 8 — Zenith of the Blue Phoenix

Tom Letanio ascends into his Zenith Form and engages Movkos in a god‑tier battle. Tom erases the Void‑God from existence, sealing the Hell Era.

Conclusion — The End of the Hell Era

With Movkos erased and the Magma‑Core Catalyst returned to its resting place, the Infernal Dimensions are sealed. The Guardians emerge stronger — but forever changed by the knowledge of the Void and the scars of the Hell Era.
