Under Your Knife

Under Your Knife is the finale of the Zavara Heist storyline. After discovering the Prism Order’s experiments and the traitor within the Guardians, Seron Darkness sends Tom Letanio on a solo mission to hunt down Vlasta Jeronim and dismantle the last remnants of the Prism Order.

1 — Seron’s Orders

Five months after the Zavara Heist, Seron calls Tom into the command chamber. The Prism Order is scattered — but not gone. And the traitor, Vlasta Jeronim, is still out there.

Seron: “Tom… this ends tonight. Find her. End the Prism Order.”

Tom nods. No hesitation. No fear. Only purpose.

2 — Descent Into the Aether Mines

Tom travels to the inner levels of Zavara’s Aether Mines, where the Prism Order carved out hidden tunnels beneath the natural crystal caverns.

The deeper he goes, the more corrupted the Aether becomes — glowing red, unstable, pulsing like a heartbeat.

Tom activates stealth mode, slipping past scanners and patrol drones.

3 — Hacking the Mainframes

Tom reaches the Prism Order’s underground facility. He hacks into the mainframes, shutting down:

• Aether mutation chambers

• Prism Order security systems

• Experimentation pods

• Communications relays

The entire base goes dark. Only emergency red lights remain.

4 — The Confrontation

In the center of the facility, Tom finds her — Vlasta Jeronim, the traitor.

She turns slowly, her expression cold.

Vlasta: “I knew Seron would send you.”

Tom: “You betrayed us. You betrayed Zavara.”

Vlasta attacks first — but Tom is faster. Aether clashes with corrupted Prism energy in a violent burst of light.

After a brutal fight, Tom overpowers her and slams her into the ground, disabling her gear.

Tom: “It’s over.”

5 — Reinforcements Arrive

Guardian Enforcer Soldiers arrive through a rift portal, securing the facility and placing Vlasta in containment cuffs.

She is transported back to Guardians HQ for interrogation.

The Prism Order is officially dead.

6 — The Meeting With Seron

Back at HQ, Tom meets with Seron in the command chamber.

Seron: “You did well, Tom. The Prism Order is finished.”

Seron pauses, then smirks slightly.

Seron: “Now that this nightmare is over… Andrew Overdark is hosting a celebration. A Blood Moon Party. He wants us there — to mark the end of the Angelic Purges.”

Tom raises an eyebrow. A party after all this?

Tom: “Guess we could use a break.”

And so begins the next chapter — The Blood Moon Party.
