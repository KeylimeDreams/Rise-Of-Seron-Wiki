The Golden Trio (1992–1999)

Before the Rift. Before the corruption. Before destiny tore them apart.

Seron Darkness, Connor Violet, and David Spike were once known across Planet Guardia as The Golden Trio — three young adults who shaped the city long before they shaped the galaxy.

1992 — The First Meeting

Seron met Connor Violet during a volunteer rescue mission in Guardia City’s East District. Connor was already a rising hero — disciplined, calm, and shield‑focused. Seron was raw potential, a natural leader even before his ascension.

David Spike joined them weeks later after stopping a street‑level gang attack alone. He was charismatic, fearless, and reckless — the spark the trio needed.

1993–1995 — The Party Years

The three became inseparable. By day, they were heroes. By night, they were legends in Guardia’s nightlife.

They hit neon clubs, rooftop parties, and underground music halls. Seron was the calm one, Connor the responsible one, and David the wild one dragging them into trouble — and out of it.

Their bond became unbreakable. Their teamwork became flawless. Their names became known across the city.

1996–1998 — The Hero Era

The trio formed an unofficial hero squad, responding to threats across Guardia:

• Seron — strategy, leadership, raw power

• Connor — defense, rescue, tactical control

• David — offense, speed, improvisation

They saved districts, stopped riots, dismantled gangs, and protected civilians. Guardia City called them: “The Three Pillars.”

1999 — The Final Year Before the Rift

Their friendship was at its strongest — and its most fragile.

David began showing signs of instability. Connor noticed first. Seron tried to help.

None of them knew the Rift was coming. None of them knew David would fall. None of them knew Seron would ascend.

But in those final months, they were still brothers — the greatest team Guardia had ever seen.
